    Nashville Xamarin User Group

    About Us

    We are aiming to build a community of Mobile C# (and F#) developers in Nashville, Tennessee, who want to help one another better understand and utilize the Xamarin toolset for building apps across multiple devices and platforms, in particular iOS and Android.

    Bring your questions, suggestions ... whatever you'd like. We look forward to helping this group grow around what we all agree is an exciting technology!

    We meet each month, generally with a short presentation or lab exercise and always with a few refreshments.

    When

    We meet on the third Tuesday each month from 6 pm to ~8:30 pm. Food and drinks provided.

    Please check the Meetup page for meeting announcements.
